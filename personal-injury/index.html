The Problems Killing Your PI Firm's Revenue

Every missed call is a missed case. Every slow follow-up is revenue walking out the door. Here's what's costing your firm the most.

📞 Missed Intake Calls

Research shows 30-40% of calls to PI firms go unanswered. Every missed call is a potential client — with an average case value of $5,000 to $50,000+ — calling the next firm on Google. After-hours, weekends, and lunch breaks are when your most valuable leads call and get voicemail.

⏱️ Slow Speed-to-Lead

The 5-minute rule is everything in PI. If you don't reach a lead within 5 minutes, your conversion rate drops by 10x. Most PI firms take hours — or days — to follow up on web form submissions, chat inquiries, and referrals. Your competitors are faster.

📋 Case Management Overhead

Your attorneys and paralegals are buried in administrative work — status updates, lien tracking, medical records requests, client check-ins. The billable work suffers. Case deadlines get tight. Client satisfaction drops because nobody has time to return calls.

🔄 Leads That Never Get Follow-up

50% of PI leads never receive a second touch. They filled out a form, maybe left a voicemail, but nobody followed up within 24 hours. That lead signed with another firm. Systematic follow-up sequences are the difference between a 5% and 15% sign rate.
