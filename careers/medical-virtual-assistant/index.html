Bilingual Medical Virtual Assistant

📍 Remote 💼 Full-time 🌍 Worldwide

About This Role

We're seeking a bilingual medical virtual assistant to support healthcare practices. You'll handle patient communications, prior authorization follow-ups, medical charting, and billing support while using AI tools to work more efficiently.

This is a remote position with full-time hours and flexibility. You'll work with cutting-edge AI tools that enhance your productivity, and you'll be part of a supportive team of US-based managers and coordinators.

Responsibilities

  • • Answer patient calls and respond to inquiries in English and Spanish
  • • Manage prior authorization requests and insurance follow-up
  • • Enter and update medical records in EMR/EHR systems (Epic, Cerner, SimplePractice, etc.)
  • • Support billing and collections tasks
  • • Schedule appointments and manage calendars
  • • Process referrals and patient intake forms
  • • Maintain HIPAA compliance and data security standards

Requirements

Required

  • • 2+ years experience in medical office administration or healthcare support
  • • EMR/EHR system experience (Epic, Cerner, athenahealth, SimplePractice, or similar)
  • • Fluent in English and Spanish (speaking and writing)
  • • Knowledge of medical billing, insurance verification, and prior authorization processes
  • • Comfortable with HIPAA regulations and patient confidentiality
  • • Reliable internet connection and quiet home office
  • • Strong phone communication skills and attention to detail

Nice to Have

  • • Familiarity with AI tools and automation
  • • Experience with GoHighLevel or other CRM platforms
  • • Medical coding or billing certification

Benefits

  • ✓ AI-powered tools provided to boost your productivity
  • ✓ US-based management and support team
  • ✓ Flexible work schedule and remote flexibility
  • ✓ Comprehensive HIPAA and system training included
  • ✓ Opportunities for advancement and growth
  • ✓ Competitive pay for your experience level
