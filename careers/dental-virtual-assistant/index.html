Dental Virtual Assistant

📍 Remote 💼 Full-time 🌍 Worldwide

About This Role

We're seeking a dental virtual assistant with experience managing dental office operations. You'll handle patient scheduling, insurance verification, appointment confirmations, and administrative support while using AI tools to boost productivity.

Remote, full-time position with flexible hours and opportunity to grow your skills with cutting-edge technology.

Responsibilities

  • • Answer patient calls and provide dental office information
  • • Schedule and reschedule dental appointments
  • • Verify insurance coverage and patient eligibility
  • • Send appointment reminders via phone, email, and text
  • • Enter patient information into practice management software
  • • Handle patient inquiries and billing questions
  • • Support administrative tasks and data entry
  • • Manage patient records and treatment plans

Requirements

Required

  • • 2+ years dental office administrative experience
  • • Dental software experience (Dentrix, Eaglesoft, etc.) or willingness to learn
  • • Insurance verification and benefits investigation experience
  • • Patient scheduling and appointment management skills
  • • Excellent phone communication skills
  • • Strong organizational abilities
  • • Reliable internet and quiet home office

Nice to Have

  • • Dental assistant certification
  • • Experience with AI tools

Benefits

  • ✓ AI tools to enhance your productivity
  • ✓ US-based management and support
  • ✓ Remote work with flexible schedule
  • ✓ Comprehensive training on software and procedures
  • ✓ Competitive compensation
