01 / Brand Identity

Who We Are

Mission

Hello Viki .AI provides AI-powered assistants that transform productivity for healthcare and law professionals — from intake to billing to patient communication.

Positioning

We sit at the intersection of AI automation and human empathy. Our brand should feel premium, modern, and trustworthy — never clinical or cold.

Voice & Tone

Confident, not arrogant. Technical, not jargony. Warm, not casual. We explain complex AI simply and make professionals feel empowered, not replaced.

Target Audience

Medical practice managers, PI law firm intake directors, healthcare administrators, and legal operations leads. They value efficiency, compliance (HIPAA), and ROI.

Brand Keywords

AI-Powered Modern Trustworthy Efficient HIPAA Compliant Premium Approachable Results-Driven

08 / Implementation

Tailwind Config

Copy into tailwind.config.js.

// tailwind.config.js
module.exports = {
  theme: {
    extend: {
      colors: {
        brand: {
          DEFAULT: '#7C3AED',
          light:   '#A78BFA',
          dark:    '#4C1D95',
          50:      '#F5F3FF',
          100:     '#EDE9FE',
        },
        accent: {
          pink:    '#EC4899',
          cyan:    '#06B6D4',
          lime:    '#65A30D',
        },
      },
      fontFamily: {
        display: ['Plus Jakarta Sans', 'Inter', 'system-ui'],
        body:    ['Inter', 'system-ui'],
        mono:    ['JetBrains Mono', 'monospace'],
      },
      borderRadius: {
        xl:    '12px',
        '2xl': '16px',
      },
    },
  },
}
